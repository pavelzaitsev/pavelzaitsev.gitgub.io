Mobile Coffee Shop

"Smile Coffee" is new and unique type of public catering in the Republic of Belarus. Our mobile coffee shop specializes only in making high quality coffee beverages using experience gained from "Lavazza" barista courses.

The project by itself includes regular car "Smart Fortwo", which is re-equipped especially for mobile coffee shop.

The business project includes two mobile coffee machines.
